My name is Sean Flavin (he/him,)I'm a graphic designer in the VIU Graphic Design program, and I’ve had an unyielding interest in aesthetics for as long as I can remember. I gravitate toward editorial work and typography—layout, hierarchy, rhythm, the stuff that looks effortless when it’s done right and looks loud when it isn’t. I’m also the kind of designer who cares about the technical side: clean files, sensible workflows, and outputs that behave the way they’re supposed to (print or screen). Film is a steady influence on my taste, not as a strict reference, more as something that’s shaped how I respond to composition and mood over time. When I’m not designing, I’m usually making music or planning a trip.

BOOKCOVER DESIGN
TITLE: CARTOON YRSELF
Book cover design for a physical print edition. The brief was to match the book’s playful, energetic tone and keep the cover feeling immediate and bold. I built the title around heavy, geometric type to maximize impact and readability, then paired it with Creative Commons imagery that I sourced and reworked to fit the composition and mood. The final cover focuses on clear hierarchy and high contrast so it holds up at a glance, on a shelf, or as a small digital thumbnail.
